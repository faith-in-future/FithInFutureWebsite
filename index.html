Advance Course

Financial Derivatives: A Simplified Overview
Futures: Contracts for buying/selling assets at a future date and price. Used for hedging and speculation.
Options: Option Chain: Lists available options. OTM, ATM, ITM: Out-of-the-money, at-the-money, in-the-money options. Value: Time value and intrinsic value. Option Greeks: Measures of risk and profit (e.g., Delta, Theta). Open Interest: Number of active contracts.
M&W Pattern Strategy: M and W patterns: Technical analysis tools for identifying potential reversals.
Option Strategies: Combining options for risk management and profit. Examples: Straddle, Strangle, Iron Condor.
Trader Mindset: Focus: Discipline, emotional control, patience. Psychology: Avoid biases, manage risk, handle stress.
